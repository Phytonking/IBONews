Welcome to the IBONews

What is this Project?

This is r/IBO Newspaper. This way, members can share their writings and their talents, such as drawing, and even music. Not only these, but this newspaper can allow members to share their stories, tips, and help one another, which is essentially the purpose of this server. This can serve as a platform for students to explore issues that are important to them and their peers, such as social justice, mental health, academic stress, and the IBO, of course. This project will not only help bring the community together, but it will also help students develop their writing, editing, and digital media skills, which can be beneficial for the diploma programme, and even possibly their future academic and professional careers. Partaking in this project can help members to promote critical thinking, awareness, and advocacy, for things they’re interested about.
